# Ayushmaan Bazar Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Model

### Commission-Based Income
1. **Insurance Company Partnerships**
   - Earn commission on successful policy issuances
   - Commission rates vary by policy type and insurer
   - Typical range: 5-25% of first-year premium

2. **Lead Generation**
   - Qualified leads submitted to insurance partners
   - Payment per lead or per conversion
   - Higher value for complete applications

### Reward Benefit Distribution
1. **Customer Cashback**
   - Fixed percentage of commission shared with customers
   - Default: 5% of policy premium (configurable)
   - Credited only after policy issuance confirmation
   - Clearly labeled as promotional benefit

2. **Compliance Framework**
   - IRDAI-compliant language throughout
   - No guaranteed return claims
   - Facilitator role clearly stated
   - Promotional nature of rewards emphasized

## Cost Structure

### Fixed Costs
- Platform development and maintenance
- Server and infrastructure costs
- Compliance and legal expenses
- Customer support operations

### Variable Costs
- Reward Benefit payouts (5% of commission)
- Marketing and user acquisition
- Payment processing fees
- Partner integration costs

## Profitability Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators
1. **Conversion Rate**: Leads to issued policies
2. **Average Commission per Policy**: Revenue per conversion
3. **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Marketing spend per user
4. **Lifetime Value**: Total commission per customer
5. **Reward Benefit Ratio**: Payout as % of commission

### Break-Even Analysis
- Target: 20% conversion rate on submitted leads
- Average commission: ₹5,000 per policy
- Reward payout: ₹250 per policy (5%)
- Net revenue: ₹4,750 per policy
- Break-even: ~200 policies per month

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: User Acquisition (Months 1-6)
- Focus on building user base
- Competitive reward percentages
- Referral program incentives
- Digital marketing campaigns

### Phase 2: Optimization (Months 7-12)
- Improve conversion rates
- Optimize partner mix
- Reduce customer acquisition costs
- Enhance user experience

### Phase 3: Expansion (Year 2+)
- Add more insurance categories
- Premium features for power users
- B2B agent partnerships
- White-label solutions

## Compliance Considerations

### IRDAI Regulations
- Maintain facilitator status (not insurer)
- Transparent commission disclosure
- No misleading reward claims
- Proper policy documentation

### Financial Transparency
- Clear terms and conditions
- Reward calculation methodology
- Payout timelines and conditions
- Dispute resolution process
